Who Are We?


Decks and decks is a small, employee owned, contracting business that cares about its clients. Reputation is everything to us and our work reflects that. Rest assured that each project we take is treated as our most important regardless of size. We do everything with the customer in mind from the initial planning, to sweeping up after work each day. We don’t leave a job a mess. Clean work is quality work. We take on a large variety of projects, big and small. Don’t see it on our site? Feel free to shoot us a message, we may surprise you.
