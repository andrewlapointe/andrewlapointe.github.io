New Deck Construction

If you want a new deck or want to completely replace an old one, we can do it! New construction is the best option for customization, longevity, and safety. If there is an existing structure we start with a careful, clean removal. Then we build exactly the deck you want. There is no such thing as a “stock” design. Each customer gets their own tailored solution. We offer a variety of styles and material types such as: pressure treated wood, weather resistant hardwood, or composite. We will do our best to get the look you want for the price you can afford.

Deck Resurfacing

Have an existing deck that is looking a bit grey? You may not need to replace it to have a “like new” appearance. Rather than go through the hassle of a new construction our team will sand back any old finish and weathering and apply out best deck stain. This will leave your deck refreshed and weatherized, ready to stand up to the elements.

Deck Repair

This is usually the most affordable option for updating the safety of an existing structure. If there are broken boards, missing/loose rails, loose posts, or minor water damage we can fix it. Most problems are fixable without a total tear down. Don’t spend more than you need!

Fence Construction

Want a new fence built? We have you covered. From privacy fences to decorative garden fences we can make it happen. We build our fences from the highest quality pressure treated wood that is rated for ground contact and use concrete to set them. Our fences can stand up to wind, rain, and years of use.

Note About Safety

Safety is number one when we build new structures and make repairs. Everything is done using best practices and the newest, code compliant, technology. When you use our services you can be confidant in the quality of construction.
